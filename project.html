Executive summary

How will 400 agile teams build on the work of each other at DB Systel? Squad is the project directory that integrates with the intranet systems of DB Systel and uses AI to provide insights and suggestions to improve success rates and reduce organizational waste.

The core idea is to have a standardized format to structure project information. This makes it easier to search through projects and makes it easier to find those projects that might be relevant to your. These questions are:

  1. What is the name of your project?
  2. Why does this project matter?
  3. What is your goal(s)?
  4. Who is on your team?
  5. What technologies did you use?
  6. Where are your code and assets stored?
  7. What other resources are relevant?
